HTML PLAY

The HTML play exercise made coding feel much more physical and spatial. Instead of looking at tags on a screen, laying them out by hand made me think more carefully about structure, hierarchy, and nesting. It turned HTML into something almost architectural.

What also stood out to me was how important color-coding and indexation was for me, on vscode. I relied on it for clarity and to quickly glance over code snippets. When there is no color and indexation, it quickly became confusing. And I was missing closing tags and mixing up elements.
