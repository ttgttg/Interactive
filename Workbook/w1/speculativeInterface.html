Schrödinger's Dog

Given object: wooden block

faces with varying roughness and textures
two small drill holes

Interface: Companionship device between owner & dog

Use case:
Locks phone into the box, and releases smell of treats to stimulate play. Pressure sensing mechanism to detect dog's energy and enthusiasm level, based on their bite strength.


Phone lock will be released, and box opens when your pet is tired/bored of the play session.
Bonus: In-built camera to capture your dog's perspective through the drill hole.


I think an interesting space is the gap between an object I know so well that I don't see it (or feel the interactivity), versus the one that makes me aware of my own hands.
